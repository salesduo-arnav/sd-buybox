# Amazon Visibility (Buy Box) Tracker
**Project Overview & Executive Summary**

## What is the Buy Box Tracker?
The Buy Box Tracker is a brand new micro-tool integrated directly into our core SaaS ecosystem. It acts as an automated 24/7 watchtower for Amazon sellers and vendors, empowering them to actively monitor the "Buy Box" status of their entire product catalog. 

On Amazon, the "Buy Box" is the coveted white box on the right side of the product detail page where customers click "Add to Cart" or "Buy Now." When a seller loses this box, their sales immediately plummet. This tool tells users **when** they lose it, **why** they lost it, and exactly **how much revenue** that loss is costing them.

## The Problem
For brands selling on Amazon, maintaining product visibility is a constant battle. Competing sellers or retail platforms can undercut your prices, or Amazon might temporarily suppress your listings. When sales mysteriously drop, brand managers are often left in the dark, forced to manually check listings to figure out what went wrong. 

## The Solution
Instead of having sellers blindly guess why daily sales are suffering, the Buy Box Tracker automatically polls Amazon throughout the day to detect issues in real-time. If the status drops, it fires off an immediate alert so the problem can be addressed before significant revenue is lost.

## Key Features & Value Proposition

### 1. Estimated Sales Missed (Revenue Impact)
Every hour a product loses the Buy Box, it loses money. We calculate the exact dollar amount of "Missed Sales" by cross-referencing the time the Buy Box was lost with the product's historical daily sales velocity. This transforms an abstract metric ("25% visibility") into a tangible financial loss ("$2,450 missed"), helping brands prioritize which issues to fix first. 

### 2. "Why Did We Lose?" Diagnostics
Losing the Buy Box is a symptom; sellers need the cure. The tool automatically categorizes the reason for the loss so brands know exactly who to fight:
* **Cheaper 3P Sellers:** A third-party competitor is currently undercutting the price.
* **Lower External Price:** Amazon found the product cheaper on another site (like Walmart or Target) and suppressed it.
* **Cannibalization (Lower Own VC/SC Price):** The brand is competing against itself because its Vendor Central and Seller Central accounts have conflicting prices.

### 3. Smart, Actionable Alerts
Brands don't have to live inside our dashboard. The tool proactively pushes intelligent alerts to Slack or Email. Sellers can choose to be disturbed only when things get truly critical—like a blockbuster product's visibility dropping below 50%.

### 4. Effortless Management
The micro-tool comes with a streamlined Settings menu. Users can decide whether to track all products automatically or just a curated VIP list. They can also define how frequently the system checks in on Amazon—ranging from highly responsive Real-Time checking to a cost-effective Daily rundown.

## Summary
The Buy Box Tracker removes the guesswork from Amazon channel management. By automating catalog monitoring, attaching real dollar values to visibility, and identifying the root causes of issues, this micro-tool equips sellers with the hard data they need to protect their territory and recover lost revenue immediately.
